Our Story

Built on a single belief: great ideas deserve great tools

Damru was founded in 2014 in Mumbai with a small team of eight engineers and one big conviction — that the most powerful technologies should be accessible to everyone, not just the few. We named ourselves after the damru, Shiva's hand-drum whose rhythm is said to have created language, music, and time itself. That sense of foundational energy is what we bring to every product we build.

Today we operate across four continents, powering communication, productivity, and commerce for millions of individuals and businesses worldwide.
